Submission Guidelines

  • Topic: Must be related to economics, business, or personal finance.
  • Tone: Write in a clear, accessible, and engaging style. Avoid overly academic jargon.
  • Originality: All submissions must be your own original work.
  • Word Count: Aim for 600-1,200 words.
  • Citations: Please hyperlink to credible sources for all statistics and data points.
  • Review Process: Our editorial team will review your submission and provide feedback within two weeks.
